Brief History About EduGlobalBank by Chanelle MFB

The Organisation was founded with the aim of helping to end the Ugly lead that Nigeria maintains as the World’s Illiteracy Headquarters and the retrogressive Statistics that puts the Number of Out 0f School Children below the Age of 15 to between 11 and 15 Million. This cannot continue especially because the World Bank says that “Education is the Biggest Key to lift People out of Generational Poverty. We also belief that “doing Good in Society can be Good Business” hence we have made a business that can sustain and eventually win the war against Illiteracy in Nigeria and Africa by Providing the Much needed Finance to drive the Education Sector in Nigeria and Africa for Sustainable Growth and much Needed Development. The Company was formed in 2014, before changing its name in 2019; The Company has Operations in each of the 36 States in Nigeria, and the FCT.

Who We Are

EduGlobalBank by Chanelle MFB is a Brand representation of Shemitah Social Ventures Ltd.RC.1581367 which arguably is Africa's First Education Ecosystem Focused Financial Services Organisation. Our Products and Services cater to Students (and their Parents), Private School Teachers (and their Non-Teaching Colleagues), and Private Schools (and their Owners).

What We Do

Our Services to Members of the Education Ecosystem range from Loans to Payments, to Savings, to Capital Markets, to Bills Payments, to Agency Banking, to Insurance, to Pension, to Mortgage, to Health Insurance. We are able to Offer all our Products and Services as a result of our Partnerships with various Licensed Entities such as Insurance Companies, Pension Fund Administrator, Capital Market Operators, etc
